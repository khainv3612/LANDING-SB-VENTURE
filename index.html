Welcome to SB Ventures - A private investment firm prioritizes early-stage investments in blockchain and cryptocurrency businesses and provides long-term support through the formation and maintenance of valuable partnerships.

Our Mission

Our Mission

SB Ventures was established in 2022 with the goal of being one of the most successful and well-known venture capital firms in the world. We highly focus on NFT Marketplace, Defi, GameFi Ecosystem, SocialFi, and Move-to-Earn.

SB Ventures' mission is to share our knowledge with potential businesses and increase development and professionalism in the cryptocurrency and Defi industry.
